Cookie Policy

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit, such as your preferences and login information. Cookies can be either "persistent" (remaining on your device until you delete them) or "session" (temporary and deleted when you close your browser).

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the basic functionality of our website, such as authentication and security
  • Performance Cookies: Used to collect information about how you use our website to help us improve its performance and functionality
  • Analytics Cookies: Allow us to understand visitor behavior and measure the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings to personalize your experience
  • Marketing Cookies: Used to deliver targeted advertising and track the success of marketing campaigns

Third-Party Cookies

We may allow third-party service providers, such as analytics platforms, advertising networks, and social media companies, to place cookies on your device. These third parties use cookies to track your behavior across websites and deliver targeted advertising. You can control third-party cookies through your browser settings or by using opt-out tools provided by these services.

Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or alert you when cookies are being sent. However, disabling cookies may affect the functionality and usability of our website. For more information about managing cookies, visit the help section of your browser or use resources like allaboutcookies.org.
