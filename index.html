About DesignPro

Founded in 2012, DesignPro emerged from a passion for creating transformative visual experiences. Our journey began with a small team of three designers working from a modest studio, driven by a singular vision: to prove that exceptional design is accessible to businesses of all sizes. Over the past twelve years, we've evolved into a full-service creative powerhouse, collaborating with over 500 brands across diverse industries including technology, finance, healthcare, retail, and entertainment. Our growth has been fueled by our unwavering commitment to understanding our clients' unique challenges and delivering design solutions that don't just look beautiful—they drive measurable business results and create lasting impressions in the minds of consumers.

At our core, we believe that great design is the intersection of strategy, creativity, and technology. Our mission is to empower businesses to stand out in an increasingly crowded marketplace by creating distinctive visual identities that resonate with their target audiences. We're committed to fostering a culture of continuous innovation, collaborative partnerships, and ethical design practices. Every project we undertake is guided by our core values: excellence in execution, transparency in communication, integrity in our work, and genuine investment in our clients' long-term success. We don't just create designs; we build lasting relationships with our clients and take pride in being an extension of their creative vision.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our graphic design services

We provide comprehensive graphic design services including:

  • Branding & Identity: Logo design, brand guidelines, visual identity systems, and complete brand overhauls
  • Web Design: Website design, UI/UX design, landing pages, and digital interfaces
  • Print Design: Business cards, letterheads, brochures, packaging, posters, and promotional materials
  • Marketing Materials: Social media graphics, email templates, infographics, and advertising design
  • Illustration & Animation: Custom illustrations, animated graphics, and motion design
  • Consultation: Brand strategy, design consultation, and creative direction

Project timelines vary depending on scope and complexity. Here's a general overview:

  • Simple Projects (logo, business cards): 1-2 weeks
  • Medium Projects (branding package, website): 3-6 weeks
  • Complex Projects (complete brand overhaul, large campaigns): 6-12 weeks
  • Rush Projects: We offer expedited services for an additional fee

We provide detailed project timelines during the initial consultation and keep you updated throughout the process with regular progress reports and milestone deliverables.

Our pricing is flexible and tailored to your specific needs and budget. We offer several engagement models:

  • Project-Based Pricing: Fixed rate for specific deliverables (best for defined projects)
  • Hourly Rates: $75-$150/hour depending on designer experience and project complexity
  • Retainer Packages: Monthly retainers starting at $1,500 for ongoing design support
  • Custom Packages: Tailored solutions for unique business needs

We provide transparent quotes with no hidden fees. During your free consultation, we'll discuss your budget and create a proposal that delivers maximum value for your investment.

We're committed to your satisfaction, which is why we include comprehensive revision rounds in our projects:

  • Included Revisions: Most projects include 2-3 rounds of revisions at no additional cost
  • Unlimited Revisions: We offer packages with unlimited revisions for a fixed price
  • Major Changes: Substantial design direction changes may incur additional fees
  • Revision Timeline: We typically deliver revision rounds within 3-5 business days

We work collaboratively with you to ensure the final design perfectly matches your vision. Your satisfaction is our top priority, and we won't consider a project complete until you're thrilled with the results.

Getting started is simple and straightforward. Here's our process:

  1. Initial Consultation: Contact us through our website or email. We'll schedule a free consultation to discuss your project, goals, and budget.
  2. Proposal & Agreement: We'll provide a detailed proposal outlining scope, timeline, deliverables, and pricing. Once approved, we'll have you sign our standard agreement.
  3. Discovery & Strategy: We'll dive deep into understanding your brand, industry, target audience, and competitive landscape.
  4. Design & Development: Our team will create initial concepts and present them for your feedback and direction.
  5. Refinement & Delivery: Based on your feedback, we'll refine the designs and deliver final files in your required formats.
